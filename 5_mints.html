Long Love Message & Poetry

Ap mera sabse pyara tohfa hain — Allah ki rehmat, meri duaon ka qubool jawab. Aaj ke is khaas pal se pehle main ap ke liye har sakoon aur har khushi ki dua karta hoon.

1. 💌 Ap meri subah ka pehla khayal hain aur raat ka aakhri sukoon.
2. 🌙 Apki muskurahat mere dil ka wo noor hai jo hamesha roshan rahe.
3. 🌹 آپ میری دعاؤں کا نور ہیں، میری دنیا کا سکون ہیں۔
4. ✨ In your eyes I find the promise of a thousand beautiful tomorrows.
5. 💫 Har saans me apka naam, har dua me apki khushi — mera sab kuch.
6. 🌷 Ap meri zindagi ka wo naghma jo dil ko chain deta hai.
7. 💞 I thank Allah for you every day — a blessing I will never take for granted.
8. 🌸 Ap ki har muskaan mere liye jannat ka ehsas hai.
9. 🌟 My heart whispers your name in every quiet moment — Urba Fatima.
10. 💖 Ap wo saaya hain jo meri zindagi ko garmayi deta hai.
11. 🕊 May Allah fill your days with iman, health, and endless joy.
12. 💐 ہر لمحہ اللہ کی رحمت آپ پر سایہ فگن رہے۔
13. ✨ My love for you is calm, deep and forever — an unspoken promise.
14. 🌹 Aap meri dua ka hissa hain jo har waqt mere saath rehta hai.
15. 💛 Forever and always — ap mere dil mein ho, meri Jaan.

Duas & Islamic Touch

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيمِ

اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لَهَا فِي عُمْرِهَا وَزادْهَا مِنْ خَيْرِكَ

Meaning: Ya Allah, apke jeene mein barkat de aur apko apni rehmat se nawaz.

Hadees:

“The best among you are those who are best to their families.” — Prophet Muhammad ﷺ

Short dua from me:

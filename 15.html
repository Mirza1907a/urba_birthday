⏳ 15 Minutes Remaining for My Queen's Birthday ❤️

محترمہ اُربا فاطمہ

اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لَهَا وَأَطِلْ عُمْرَهَا فِي طَاعَتِكَ وَاجْعَلْهَا قُرَّةَ عَيْنٍ لِي

🌹 "Ap meri duaon ka wo hissa hain, jo kabhi qubool hone se rukta nahi."
🌹 "Ap meri rooh ka wo sukoon hain jo har sajde mein mehsoos hota hai."
🌹 "Ap ke bina meri subah adhoori, ap ke bina meri raat adhuri."
🌹 "Ap meri har dua ka pehla jumla, aur har dua ka aakhri lafz hain."
🌹 "Jab ap muskuraati hain, lagta hai jannat yahan hi utar aayi hai."
🌹 "Ap meri duniya ka wo noor hain jo har andheray ko roshan karta hai."
🌹 "Meri mohabbat ki misaal sirf apka naam hai."
🌹 "Ap meri rooh ka wo naghma hain jo hamesha chalti dhadkanon mein gungunata hai."
🌹 "Allah ne mujhe jo sabse khoobsurat tohfa diya, wo ap hain."
🌹 "Ap meri duaon ka wo raaz hain jo sirf Rab jaanta hai."
🌹 "Ap meri har khushi ka sabab, har gham ka marham hain."
🌹 "Meri zindagi ka har safha, ap ke bina khali hai."
🌹 "Ap meri mohabbat ka wo paigham hain jo kabhi purana nahi hota."
🌹 "Ap meri duaon ki wo lakeer hain jo hamesha mustaqeem rehti hai."
🌹 "Ap meri rooh ki dua, meri aankhon ka sapna, aur meri zindagi ka noor hain."
